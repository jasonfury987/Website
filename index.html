Our Virtual Professionals

⁖ Pre-Trained

REVA Global Virtual Assistants undergo an intensive 4-week real estate training program, equipping them with the knowledge and skills to seamlessly integrate into your team and contribute to your success.

⁖ College Educated & Highly Experienced

REVA Global Virtual Assistants hold 4-year college degrees and possess extensive professional experience, ensuring they bring both academic excellence and practical expertise to your team.

⁖ Custom Placement Process

Our team will match you with a dedicated Virtual Assistant whose skill set precisely aligns with your specific needs.

⁖ Client Services Managers

With REVA Global, you gain not only a highly skilled VA but also a dedicated Client Services Manager (CSM). Your CSM oversees the daily activities of your VA, ensuring they stay on track and adhere to your goals and processes. You’ll have full access to your CSM for any questions, adjustments, or feedback, providing you with seamless support and peace of mind.

⁖ DISC Profile & Predictive Index Assesment

All of our Virtual Assistants undergo assessment using the DISC Profile and Predictive Index methods. These personality tests help us identify the roles in which your Virtual Assistant will excel, ensuring the best fit for your needs.

⁖ Neutral Accents

Our Virtual Assistants are based in the Philippines and possess strong proficiency in English, an official language there. They have minimal to no accent and boast excellent communication skills, ensuring clear and effective interactions.

⁖ 100% Dedicated To Your Business

When you hire a NEXIS Global Virtual Assistant, they become fully dedicated to your business, working exclusively for you. Moonlighting is strictly prohibited, ensuring their complete focus on your needs.

⁖ Cultural Match

Our Virtual Assistants share core cultural values with Americans, including a strong emphasis on family, education, and loyalty.
